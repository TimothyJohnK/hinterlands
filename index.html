The Hinterlands

is

Paul Fonfara (clarinet, whistling, voice) and Tim Kirchhof (guitar, voice, textures)

The Hinterlands weave of a tapestry of stories through music. The styles they play are many: trad-jazz, Dixieland, old-timey cowboy songs, heartfelt blues, songs of the field and prairie and songs of the mountains and the swamps. They play American music.
